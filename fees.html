Rental Options & Rates

  • Conference Seating ()
    • Access to main hall
    • Includes use of tables and chairs
    • Maximum capacity: 100 people
    • Note: No food service or food preparation allowed; no food prep room access.
  • Banquet Seating ()
    • Access to main hall
    • Includes access to food preparation area
    • Includes use of tables and chairs
    • Maximum capacity: 100 people
  • Garden Use ()
    • Access to outdoor garden area only
    • Maximum capacity: 200 people
    • Note: No access to building or interior facilities.

Additional Fees & Deposits

  • Dumpster Use (): Optional add-on, but required for events generating significant waste.
  • Security Deposit & Cleaning Fee (): $150 is retained as a standard cleaning fee. $100 is refundable provided there is no damage and no excessive cleaning is required.
  • Booking Deposit: 50% of the total rental fee is due at the time of booking. This is non-refundable within 7 days of the event.
