Welcome to the Creston Community Center

Welcome to the Creston Community Center! For over 30 years, we have proudly served as the heart of Creston—a dedicated gathering place where our town comes together. Managed by C.A.T.C.H., our facility provides an essential meeting space for the local organizations, clubs, and civic groups that make our community so special.

Whether you are planning a wedding reception, a family reunion, a town hall meeting, or a private party, our hall is available to rent for your special occasions. Guests will also love exploring the amazing, all-volunteer Creston Community Garden located right next door, offering a beautiful and peaceful backdrop for any event.
